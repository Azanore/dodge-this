HOW TO PLAY

You
Your ship move with your mouse — don't get hit
Obstacles — avoid all of them
Ball steady, predictable
Bullet fast and straight
Shard unpredictable angle
Tracker hunts you — only screenclear removes it
Score zone
Green zone enter to build your multiplier — score banks when it drops to x1
Power-ups — collect them
Slow-mo slows all obstacles
Shield temporary invincibility
Clear removes all obstacles instantly
Shrink smaller hitbox

click outside or press Escape to close
